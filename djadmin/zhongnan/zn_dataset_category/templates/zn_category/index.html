January 7, 2025 Shigatse earthquake, China

Overview

According to the China Earthquake Networks Center, a 6.8-magnitude earthquake occurred in Dingri County, Shigatse City, Xizang Autonomous Region, at 9:05 AM on January 7, 2025. The earthquake had a focal depth of 10 kilometers, with its epicenter located at 28.50°N and 87.45°E.

Disaster Monitoring Products

Figure. 1 Distribution Map of Earthquake Damaged Buildings in Dingri County, Shigatse City, Xizang Autonomous Region (Cartographer: Wang Wei, Liu Kun, Wu Lixin)

Fig.1 reveals that the buildings in the epicentral area and surrounding towns and villages have generally suffered varying degrees of damage, indicating the extensive reach of the seismic wave and the unevenness of its destructive power.
